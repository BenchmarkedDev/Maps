Contamination

Released 01/01/01

My most recent map and the first map that I've released for years. With gameplay designed to be friendly to beginners, while still having competitive routes for advanced players, this map offers enjoyment to players of all skill levels.

Adding on high attention to detail with the design of each and every room, hidden lore behind the origin of the map, and eerie ambience - this map is by far my best yet in terms of visuals.

Benchpressed

Released 27/08/21

A map that combines various segments of dozens of some of my most well-known maps, and by far my longest linear map so far.

This map stands out due to its incredibly varied gameplay and visuals. It's easy to beat, but extremely hard to get a good no-fail run. This was also the final map I released before I stopped mapping for years.
