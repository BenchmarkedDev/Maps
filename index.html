Who I am

I'm a level designer best known by the alias "Benchmarked". I am primarily known for my Bunny Hop maps, although I have made maps for a number of other gamemodes such as Prop Hunt, Murder, Surf, and Sandbox.

Bunny Hop still remains the gamemode in which I have made my most contributions though, with over 60 maps currently published.

What I do

I create maps for all source games, but mainly CSS. I aspire to make my maps beatable for inexperienced players, while also making them competitive for more advanced players to strive for better times.

I enjoy the process of having an idea in your head, and slowly bringing it to life inside of Hammer. I don't map too often anymore, though.

How it started

I always had an interest in level design, but never knew how to actually make a map. I was an Admin on a Garry's Mod Prop Hunt server, and was one day asked to go to the Bunny Hop server to moderate the players.

I got into the gamemode and met a bunch of cool people, made my first (terrible) map, and that's really where my mapping journey began.
